Bousra waterfall

BouSra Waterfall, also known as Bousra or Busra Waterfall, is a popular three-tiered waterfall located in Mondulkiri Province, Cambodia, about 43 kilometers from Sen Monorom, and is a popular destination for tourists and locals.

Sen Monorom waterfall

Sen Monorom Waterfall is located about 2 kilometres from the. provincial capital, Sen Monorom, and is a popular spot for swimming and picnicking. The waterfall is surrounded beautiful forest and is a great place to relax and enjoy the natural beauty of Mondulkiri.
